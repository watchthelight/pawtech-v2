## What is PawtropolisMC?

PawtropolisMC is a 24/7 Minecraft server running on version 1.18.2. Its purpose is based on a prison economy system, where you mine blocks in order to gain currency. You can use this currency toward our current and future features that come to the server. The goal is to envision some of our past lore when we release things such as our story mode or SMP. This area will act as our main hub to control all players. As an owner who keeps themselves open-minded, we will never induce gambling. This is in reference to crate keys. This will NEVER be a thing sold on a future store. Our goal with a future shop is to add things that will not push P2W, and a portion of the revenue will be used to support our local artists in Pawtropolis. 

This is a way to provide entertainment and support our community members.

When you attempt to connect to the server, you may be prompted to DM a bot with a code. This bot can be found in our **Minecraft ** Discord Server, which you are required to join in order to connect to the server.

# Mental Health <:heart_support:1166835434454794240>

lacks experience can have unintended negative consequences for both parties. As someone currently immersed in the health field 
and studying psychology, I understand firsthand the challenges of being a support therapist and maintaining a high standard of care.

I encourage open discussions, positive interactions, and mutual support within our broader community.

## List of Coping Mechanisms 
*Here's a list of common coping mechanisms. Please note that these are mechanisms that are verified by real psychologists, but should be used to your preference. Some coping mechanisms may not work as others.*

### Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT):

- Identify and challenge negative thought patterns.
- Develop healthier thinking habits.

### Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction (MBSR):

- Practice mindfulness meditation and awareness.
- Focus on the present moment without judgment.

### Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT):

- Learn skills such as mindfulness, emotion regulation, and interpersonal effectiveness.
- Balance acceptance and change-oriented strategies.

### Self-Compassion:

- Cultivate self-compassion and treat yourself with kindness.
- Practice self-acceptance and understanding.

### Gratitude Journaling:

- Regularly write down things you're grateful for.
- Foster a positive outlook on life.

### Social Support:

- Build and maintain strong social connections.
- Seek support from friends, family, or support groups.

### Emotion Regulation Techniques:

- Identify and label emotions accurately.
- Develop healthy ways to manage and express emotions.

### Biofeedback:

- Use technology to monitor and gain control over physiological responses (e.g., heart rate, muscle tension).
- Learn to manage stress through self-regulation.

### Positive Psychology Interventions:

- Focus on strengths and positive experiences.
- Engage in activities that bring joy and fulfillment.

### Breathing Exercises:

- Practice deep breathing and diaphragmatic breathing.
- Use controlled breathing techniques to reduce anxiety.

### Progressive Muscle Relaxation (PMR):

- Systematically tense and relax muscle groups to release physical tension.
- Promote a state of relaxation.

### Behavioral Activation:

- Increase engagement in positive and rewarding activities.
- Counteract the effects of withdrawal and isolation.

### Assertiveness Training:

- Develop assertiveness skills to express thoughts and feelings effectively.
- Set boundaries and communicate needs.
### Problem-Solving Skills:

- Break down problems into manageable parts.
- Develop practical and effective solutions.

### Sleep Hygiene:

- Establish a consistent sleep routine.
- Prioritize sufficient and quality sleep.

### Journaling for Emotional Expression:

- Write about thoughts and feelings as a way of processing emotions.
- Gain insights into patterns and triggers.

### Professional Counseling and Psychotherapy:

> A Byte is an item that can be redeemed to boost your leveling experience. They will be only available through giveaways & rewards for the time being. As it gains more popularity, we will add them to the @Paw Bank shop for purchase.

`Byte:` A "personal" token used to boost leveling experience. This can be redeemable and only applies to your profile
`AllByte:` A token that applies "globally" throughout the community. 

**Every Byte will have the same or a different multiplier level and duration. Each byte also comes with a rarity**

`Bytes have 5 rarity types:` 

[ <:ByteCommon:1381901926190420008> ] - Common Byte
- *The most common Byte Token.* It has a 2x multiplier and can last 12 hours.
[ <:ByteRare:1381901931735289856> ] - Rare Byte
- *If you look hard enough, you may find yourself a rare byte.* They can have a 3x multiplier and last up to 24 hours. 
[ <:ByteEpic:1381901930061762570> ] - Epic Byte
- *People used to mine a bunch of these in the past which makes them so rare now, but they're out there, falling out of the sky.* It last for 48 hours and has a 5x multiplier.
[ <:ByteLegendary:1381901928111538206> ] - Legendary Byte
- *Legends say there was none left on the planet, but people kept searching and rumors spread.* It can last up to 72 hours and have a 5x multiplier. 
[ <:ByteMythic:1381886074586005574> ] - Mythic Byte 
*No one has seen one before and rumors almost say it doesn't exist. There's nothing out there that isn't hidden..* Almost a myth but some have also said it lasts up to a whole week, and has a 10x multiplier. However, that's up to speculation. 

**AllBytes can only be found in Epic or Higher rarites, but have the same levels as regular Bytes.**
